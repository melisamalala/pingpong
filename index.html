Rules:

To start off, Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. The numbers that are divisible by 3 will become "ping"!
  2. The numbers that are divisible by 5 will become "pong"!
  3. The numbers that are divisible by 3 and 5 will become "pingpong!"
